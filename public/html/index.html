Welcome to Jay Jani & Associates

An alliance of Chartered Accountants

The firm Jay Jani & Associates was set up in the year 2018 by CA Jay Kumar Jani who has now more than 8 years of experience.

Jay Jani & Associates strives to provide quality services in the areas of Statutory Audit, Internal Audit, Accounting outsourcing, Taxation (Direct and Indirect), Company Law Matters for both domestic and foreign companies, Management Consultancy, Fixed Assets Verification and FEMA.

The firm is well geared to take up any professional assignments in the above areas. It has association with firms of Chartered Accountants, Management Consultants, Company Secretaries and IT Professionals etc. to provide complete range of services.

The firm is in the panel of cooperative society for major audit undertaking.

In line with the Mission Statement, we always strive to acquire more and more knowledge in such ever-changing laws.


Our Services

We cover large varities of services

1

Business Start Up

We offer comprehensive services for business formation, including LLP, Company, OPC, and Partnership. Our expertise extends to legal drafting, navigating Special Economic Zone regulations, and providing business advisory. Additionally, we assist start-ups with various registrations required under different laws to ensure compliance and smooth operations.

2

Audits

We conduct tax audits, internal and statutory audits for Indian companies, and handle operations within India. Our services also include stock and book debt audits, system audits, investigations, special audits as required by management, and fixed assets verification, ensuring thorough financial oversight and compliance.

3

Taxation

We provide comprehensive tax planning and return filing services for individuals, HUFs, firms, companies, and trusts. Our expertise includes transfer pricing certification under the Indian Income Tax Act, 1961, income tax consultancy, indirect tax advisory services, and representing clients before tax authorities to ensure compliance and optimal tax strategies.

4

Certification

We offer 15CB certification, net worth certificates, turnover certificates, and other essential CA certifications, ensuring accurate financial documentation and compliance with regulatory requirements.

5

Company Law Matters

We provide comprehensive services for company incorporation, consultancy on Company Law matters, and strategic planning for mergers, acquisitions, demergers, and corporate reorganizations. Our expertise includes filing annual returns, Clause 49 compliance, handling secretarial matters like share transfers, maintaining statutory records, advising on public/rights/bonus issues, and managing changes in name, objects, and registered office.

6

Fund Raising

Financing is crucial at every stage of a business lifecycle. We develop detailed forecast models to determine the ideal and minimum funding required, considering various sensitivity scenarios. Our expertise ensures the best deal for clients by negotiating with all parties involved. Services include preparing bank loan applications, project reports, CMA data, working capital arrangements, term loans, project finance, bank guarantees, loans against property, and collateral-free loans.

7

Foreign Investment

We offer comprehensive consultancy on Overseas Direct Investment (ODI) compliance, ensuring that businesses meet all necessary regulatory requirements. Our services include advisory on the review of foreign contracts and work orders to mitigate risks, and consultancy related to foreign financials, helping businesses navigate complex international financial landscapes with confidence and precision.

8

GST

We offer a comprehensive suite of GST services, including registration, preparation, and filing of returns. Our expertise also encompasses representation before authorities and strategic advisory, providing a holistic solution for all GST-related requirements. With our support, businesses can ensure compliance and efficiency in their GST operations seamlessly.

9

TDS

We provide expert advice on all aspects of TDS/TCS compliance, including obtaining Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN) and conducting periodic reviews of TDS/Withholding Tax compliance. Our services cover monthly TDS computation, reconciliation of TDS due and deposited, issuance of monthly and annual TDS certificates, filing of quarterly E-TDS/Manual returns, correction statements, and other related services to ensure thorough and accurate tax management.
